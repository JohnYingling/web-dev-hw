Local acts I've caught even just in short travels:

DIRTY FINGERS

Dirty Fingers are a so called "spazz punk" band from Shanghai. They yelp and wail and break stuff, and don't really care what anyone thinks. If you're looking for some seriously solid new tunes, this is your best bet. Shanghai is, in the grand scheme, a much smaller city for rock than Beijing, so you're best bet at the moment is this new startup of crazy folks!
See 'em!'

HIPERSON (海朋森) from Chengdu, Sichuan

I first caught Hiperson back in 2014, when I was on tour with P.K.14. They absolutely blew my mind, were super nice to me, and opened my eyes to Sichuan province like I never expected. We've become good friends, stayed in contact, and now, they're poised to take over not only Sichuna province, but China, and beyond! They toured Europe, and it was a success, so I can only see better and more broad things happening, as their sound shifts. They also played Beijing recently, and scrapped their entire old set for new material!
Now THAT'S a bold move, my friends.
